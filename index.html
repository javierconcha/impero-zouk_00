Who we are

We are a welcoming community of Brazilian Zouk lovers trying to spread this unique couple dance in Rome and throughout Italy since 2019. Our community leaders are Javi, Amanda and Marilena.
Brazilian Zouk is a couple dance with roots in the Lambada from Brazil and influenced by several other dances, including Brazilian Bolero, salsa, west coast swing, hip-hop, jazz, and contact improv.

What we do

We organize different activities and participate in events within Italy and Europe.

Our big event is SHARomE, hosted annually in November. We bring together local and international teachers to create a mini festival.
